Malicious URL Detector

A learning based approach to classifying Web sites into 3 classes: Safe (Benign), Malware and Malicious. The mechanism analyzes the Uniform Resource Locator itself without accessing the content of Web sites, eliminating the run-time latency . By employing learning algorithms, this scheme achieves better performance on generality and coverage compared with blacklisting service.
